Ultimate Frisbee

What is it?

  • It's a sport that takes elements from football, soccer, and basketball where instead of using a ball, a disc is used.
  • It's played on a field similar to that of a football field.
  • Each team has seven people. One team will start on offense while the other starts on defense. Both teams will begin on opposite end zones.
  • A 'kick-off' starts the game off with the team on defense pulling the disc to the team on offense.
  • Members of the offensive team must move the disc towards the defense's end zone by passing the disc to one another.
  • Once a team member has the disc, he/she cannot move until he/she passes the disc to another teammate. Otherwise, travelling occurs and the disc is sent back to the person who travelled.
  • Teammates on defense try to prevent the other team from scoring by either intercepting the thrown disc, forcing bad passes, or by stall counting to 10.
  • One person on defense can come up to a person who has the disc and stall count them up to 10 and try to block the disc from this same person.
  • This person cannot touch the person who has the disc or a foul is called. However, if they manage to count to 10 before the person throws the disc, it is a turnover.
  • If a turnover occurs, the defense now becomes the offense and vice versa. Now the new offense team works the disc the opposite way to try and score.
